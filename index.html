Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a credit card for the security deposit. International drivers must also provide an International Driving Permit.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made 48 hours before pickup are fully refundable. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

What is your age requirement?

Drivers must be at least 25 years old to rent our vehicles. For certain luxury models, the minimum age requirement may be higher.

  • Standard vehicles: 25+ years
  • Luxury vehicles: 30+ years
  • Supercars: 35+ years
  • Additional fees may apply for younger drivers

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our customer service team.

  1. Select your desired vehicle
  2. Choose pickup and return dates
  3. Fill in your personal details
  4. Select additional services
  5. Complete payment

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before pickup. Changes may be subject to availability and additional charges.

  • Date changes: Subject to availability
  • Vehicle changes: May incur upgrade fees
  • Location changes: May incur transfer fees
  • Additional services can be added anytime

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers for corporate clients.

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • Major debit cards
  • Bank transfers (corporate only)
  • Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

What is the security deposit?

A security deposit is required for all rentals and varies based on the vehicle type. It's fully refundable upon return if no damage is incurred.

  • Standard vehicles: 1,000
  • Luxury vehicles: 2,000
  • Supercars: 5,000
  • Refunded within 5-7 business days

What insurance coverage is included?

All rentals include comprehensive insurance coverage with a standard excess. Additional coverage options are available.

  • Comprehensive coverage included
  • Standard excess: 1,000
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Optional excess reduction available

Can I use my own insurance?

Yes, you can use your own insurance, but it must meet our minimum coverage requirements and be approved by our insurance team.

  • Must be fully comprehensive
  • Must cover rental vehicles
  • Must meet minimum coverage limits
  • Must be approved by our team
